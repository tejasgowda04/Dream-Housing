Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our interior design services

How long does a typical interior design project take? +

The timeline varies based on project scope. A standard residential interior takes 6-12 weeks from design to completion. Commercial projects may take 8-16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

What is included in your interior design package? +

Our comprehensive package includes consultation, space planning, 3D visualization, material selection, project management, execution with skilled labor, quality checks, and final handover. We handle everything from concept to completion.

Do you provide warranty for your work? +

Yes, we offer a 2-year warranty on all our interior workmanship and a 1-year warranty on materials. We provide detailed warranty documentation upon project completion.

Can you work within my budget? +

Absolutely. We tailor our designs to fit various budgets while maintaining quality. During our consultation, we discuss your budget and provide transparent costing with multiple options.

Do you handle permits and approvals? +

Yes, we assist with all necessary permits and approvals for structural modifications. Our team is experienced with local building codes and regulations in Bangalore.

What areas in Bangalore do you serve? +

We serve all areas of Bangalore including Whitefield, Indiranagar, Koramangala, Jayanagar, Malleshwaram, Hebbal, and surrounding regions. We also take up projects in nearby cities.

Our Design Philosophy

At Dream Housing Interiors, we believe that exceptional interior design is a harmonious blend of aesthetics, functionality, and personal expression. Every space we create tells a unique story.

Founded by Sharan Shetty, our Bangalore-based studio has been transforming residential and commercial spaces into inspiring environments since 2015. We approach each project with fresh creativity while maintaining our commitment to quality and timely delivery.

Our team of designers, architects, and craftsmen work collaboratively to ensure that every detail reflects our clients' vision while adding our signature touch of modern elegance.
